Our Story

A Passion for
Perfection

Born from a deep reverence for culinary artistry, Lumiere was founded by Chef Marcus Ashford with a singular vision - to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. Every dish is a canvas, every ingredient a brushstroke.

We source only the finest seasonal ingredients from local farms and trusted purveyors, transforming them into dishes that honor tradition while embracing modern innovation. At Lumiere, dining is not just a meal - it is a memory.
